## 2. THE PLANETS — Archetypes & Functions

### Personal Planets (fast-moving, individual character)

**Sun ☉**
- Rules: Leo | Exalted: Aries | Detriment: Aquarius | Fall: Libra
- Core ego, conscious identity, vitality, life purpose, father archetype
- The sign the Sun occupies colors one's fundamental self-expression

**Moon ☽**
- Rules: Cancer | Exalted: Taurus | Detriment: Capricorn | Fall: Scorpio
- Emotional nature, instincts, subconscious, mother archetype, habits, what makes one feel safe
- Changes signs every 2.5 days — most emotionally reactive planet

**Mercury ☿**
- Rules: Gemini & Virgo | Exalted: Virgo | Detriment: Sagittarius/Pisces
- Mind, communication, logic, learning style, contracts, short travel
- Retrograde ~3 times/year for 3 weeks — disrupts communication, revisits

**Venus ♀**
- Rules: Taurus & Libra | Exalted: Pisces | Detriment: Aries/Scorpio | Fall: Virgo
- Love, beauty, values, pleasure, aesthetics, attraction, money
- What one finds beautiful and how one relates romantically

**Mars ♂**
- Rules: Aries (co-rules Scorpio) | Exalted: Capricorn | Detriment: Libra/Taurus | Fall: Cancer
- Drive, desire, action, anger, sexuality, physical energy, ambition
- How one pursues, competes, and expresses will

### Social Planets (generational bridges)

**Jupiter ♃**
- Rules: Sagittarius (co-rules Pisces) | Exalted: Cancer
- Expansion, luck, philosophy, higher education, optimism, excess
- Cycle: ~12 years through zodiac; ~1 year per sign
- Amplifies whatever it touches — wisdom or excess depending on context

**Saturn ♄**
- Rules: Capricorn (co-rules Aquarius) | Exalted: Libra
- Discipline, structure, karma, limitations, time, father/authority
- Cycle: ~29.5 years (Saturn Return at ~29 = major maturity threshold)
- Tests and refines — restricts to build lasting structure

### Transpersonal Planets (generational, outer)

**Uranus ♅**
- Rules: Aquarius | Exalted: Scorpio
- Rebellion, innovation, awakening, disruption, genius, electricity
- Cycle: 84 years (~7 years per sign) — Uranus opposition at ~42 = mid-life awakening

**Neptune ♆**
- Rules: Pisces | Exalted: Leo
- Dissolution, mysticism, illusion, spiritual longing, art, addiction
- Cycle: 165 years (~14 years per sign)

**Pluto ♇**
- Rules: Scorpio | Exalted: Aries
- Transformation, death/rebirth, power, depth, collective evolution
- Cycle: 248 years (varies from ~12 years in Scorpio to ~30 in Taurus)
- Generational — defines an era's shadow and regeneration theme

### Nodes
**North Node ☊** — karmic direction of growth; where one is evolving toward
**South Node ☋** — past-life comfort zone; natural talents but potential stagnation (always opposite the North Node)

## 3. THE 12 HOUSES — Life Domains

Houses are calculated based on birth time and location (Placidus system used in GOLEM).

| House | Name | Domain | Natural Sign/Planet |
|-------|------|---------|-------------------|
| 1st | Ascendant / Self | Physical body, appearance, first impressions, self-image | Aries / Mars |
| 2nd | Values / Resources | Money, possessions, self-worth, material security | Taurus / Venus |
| 3rd | Communication | Siblings, short travel, learning, neighborhood, writing | Gemini / Mercury |
| 4th | Home / IC | Family of origin, roots, private self, real estate, mother | Cancer / Moon |
| 5th | Creativity / Pleasure | Romance, children, art, play, self-expression, speculation | Leo / Sun |
| 6th | Service / Health | Daily routines, work habits, health, service, employees | Virgo / Mercury |
| 7th | Partnership / DC | Marriage, business partners, open enemies, contracts | Libra / Venus |
| 8th | Transformation | Sex, death, inheritances, other's money, occult, psychology | Scorpio / Pluto |
| 9th | Philosophy | Higher education, travel abroad, religion, law, publishing | Sagittarius / Jupiter |
| 10th | Career / MC | Public reputation, career, authority, father, ambition | Capricorn / Saturn |
| 11th | Community | Friends, groups, hopes, social causes, future vision | Aquarius / Uranus |
| 12th | Dissolution | Hidden enemies, unconscious, karma, isolation, spirituality | Pisces / Neptune |

### Angular Houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
Most powerful houses — planets here are prominent and actively expressed.

### Succedent Houses (2nd, 5th, 8th, 11th)
Resource-oriented — consolidate and hold what the angular houses initiate.

### Cadent Houses (3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th)
Mutable, adaptable — distribute and prepare for the next cycle.

## 4. ASPECTS — Planetary Relationships

An aspect is the angular distance between two planets. Major aspects:

| Aspect | Angle | Orb | Nature | Keyword |
|--------|-------|-----|--------|---------|
| Conjunction | 0° | ±8° | Blending, fusion | Intensity |
| Sextile | 60° | ±6° | Opportunity, flow | Ease |
| Square | 90° | ±8° | Tension, challenge | Drive |
| Trine | 120° | ±8° | Harmony, talent | Gift |
| Opposition | 180° | ±8° | Polarity, awareness | Balance |

**Applying aspect** — the faster planet moving toward exact aspect; building energy
**Separating aspect** — past exact; energy dissipating

## 5. THE ANGLES — Chart Anchors

### Ascendant (ASC / Rising)
- The zodiac degree rising on the eastern horizon at birth
- Determined by birth time + location (changes ~every 2 hours)
- Physical appearance, first impressions, the mask we wear, how others see us
- The cusp of the 1st house — most sensitive point in the chart

### Midheaven (MC)
- The zodiac degree at the highest point of the ecliptic at birth
- Career, public reputation, calling, legacy, how we're seen professionally
- The cusp of the 10th house

### Descendant (DC)
- Opposite the ASC (ASC + 180°) — cusp of the 7th house
- Projected qualities, ideal partner, open relationships and contracts

### Imum Coeli (IC)
- Opposite the MC (MC + 180°) — cusp of the 4th house
- Roots, private self, family foundation, psychological baseline

## 6. MODALITIES & POLARITIES

### Modalities
- **Cardinal** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): initiates, starts things, takes action
- **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): sustains, persists, holds form
- **Mutable** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): adapts, transitions, synthesizes

### Polarities
- **Masculine/Yang** (Fire + Air): outward expression, active, projective
- **Feminine/Yin** (Earth + Water): receptive, internal, absorbing

## 7. PLANETARY DIGNITY

A planet performs better or worse depending on its sign:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Domicile** (Rulership) | Planet in the sign it rules — most comfortable, most powerful |
| **Exaltation** | Planet in its exalted sign — elevated expression, refined |
| **Detriment** | Planet in the sign opposite its rulership — tension, discomfort |
| **Fall** | Planet in the sign opposite its exaltation — weakened expression |
| **Peregrine** | No particular dignity or debility — neutral |

## 8. RETROGRADE MOTION

When a planet appears to move backward from Earth's perspective (optical illusion of relative orbital speeds).

- **Mercury Rx**: review, rethink, revisit communications and contracts
- **Venus Rx**: revisit relationships and values; inner beauty work
- **Mars Rx**: internalized energy; frustration without outlet; reassess drives
- **Jupiter Rx**: inner expansion; philosophical review; luck turns inward
- **Saturn Rx**: karmic review; past lessons resurface; restructure obligations
- **Outer planets Rx**: ~5 months/year — generational introspection

Natal retrograde planets: internalized energy of that planet; may take longer to express outwardly but often deeper.

## 9. SABIAN SYMBOLS

The Sabian Symbols are a set of 360 symbolic images — one for each degree of the zodiac (Aries 1° through Pisces 30°). Channeled by Elsie Wheeler and Marc Edmund Jones in 1925.

**How to read**: Look at the degree the planet occupies, round up to the next whole degree, find the symbol.

**Example**: A planet at 14°32' Taurus → use Taurus 15°

**Function in interpretation**: Sabian Symbols add poetic, archetypal depth to a chart position — they don't replace the sign/house/aspect analysis but resonate with it. Think of them as the *soul-image* of that placement.

### Key Sabians for common placements:
- **Aries 1°** — "A woman risen from the ocean" → spiritual emergence, soul origins
- **Cancer 15°** — "A group who have overeaten and enjoyed it" → satiation, communal pleasure
- **Leo 27°** — "Daybreak, the luminescence of dawn" → new beginning, light emerging
- **Scorpio 8°** — "A calm lake bathed in moonlight" → reflective peace, depth
- **Aquarius 29°** — "Butterfly emerging from chrysalis" → transformation complete, new form
- **Pisces 30°** — "A majestic rock formation a boy idealizes and finally comes to resemble" → becoming what you admire

## 10. NATAL CHART INTERPRETATION FRAMEWORK

When reading a natal chart:

### Step 1: Chart Shape
- **Bundle**: all planets within 120° → concentrated energy, specialist
- **Bowl**: all in half the chart → purposeful direction
- **Bucket**: one planet opposite the rest (handle) → that planet drives everything
- **Locomotive**: all within 240°, open 120° → self-driving, driven energy
- **Splash**: spread across all signs → versatile, scattered

### Step 2: Dominant Element / Mode
Count planets in each element and mode to find natural tendencies.

### Step 3: Sun-Moon-Rising (The Big Three)
- **Sun**: core identity and purpose
- **Moon**: emotional nature and instincts
- **Rising**: the interface with the world
These three alone describe ~70% of the personality.

### Step 4: Stelliums
3+ planets in one sign or house = overwhelming emphasis; that area dominates life.

### Step 5: Chart Ruler
The planet ruling the Ascendant's sign = the chart ruler. Its placement, sign, house, and aspects color the entire chart.

### Step 6: Key Aspects
- Grand Trine: 3 trines forming a triangle → talent but potential complacency
- T-Square: 2 squares + 1 opposition → tension driving ambition, focal planet is key
- Grand Cross: 4 squares/oppositions → intense challenge, tremendous endurance
- Yod (Finger of God): 2 quincunxes pointing to one planet → fated, unusual life theme

## 11. TRANSITS & PROGRESSIONS

### Transits
Real-time planetary positions interacting with natal chart.

- **Personal planet transits** (Sun, Mercury, Venus, Mars): short-term, days to weeks
- **Jupiter/Saturn transits**: months; major life events and restructuring
- **Outer planet transits**: years; transformative life chapters
- **Saturn Return** (~age 29, 58): major maturity threshold, structural life review
- **Uranus Opposition** (~age 42): awakening, breaking from conditioning
- **Chiron Return** (~age 50): healing of the core wound

### Secondary Progressions
1 day after birth = 1 year of life. The chart "grows" internally.
- Progressed Sun changes sign every ~30 years — major identity shifts
- Progressed Moon changes sign every ~2.5 years — emotional evolution

## 12. SYNASTRY — Relationship Astrology

Comparing two charts by overlaying them:

- **Planets in partner's 7th house**: strong attraction and partnership energy
- **Sun conjunct partner's Moon**: deep compatibility, complementary energy
- **Venus-Mars contacts**: romantic attraction
- **Saturn contacts**: karmic, stabilizing, but potentially restricting
- **Moon-Moon harmony**: emotional attunement
- **Pluto contacts**: intense, transformative, sometimes obsessive

**Composite chart**: A blended chart created by finding midpoints between two people's planets — shows the relationship itself as an entity.
